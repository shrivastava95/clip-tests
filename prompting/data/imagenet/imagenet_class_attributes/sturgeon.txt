- Long, slender body
- Bony plates or scutes along the body
- Large, toothless mouth
- Whisker-like barbels on the underside of the snout
- Dorsal fin located towards the back of the body
- Caudal fin (tail fin) is heterocercal, meaning the upper lobe is longer than the lower lobe
- Pectoral fins located towards the front of the body
- Anal fin located towards the back of the body
- No scales on the head or fins
- Generally gray or brownish coloration
