- Large head with a distinct beak-like snout
- Strong and muscular front flippers
- Brownish or reddish-brown carapace (shell) with a heart-shaped appearance
- Rough and bumpy texture on the carapace
- Lighter-colored plastron (underside of the shell)
- Five pairs of large scales on the top of the carapace
- Claws on the front flippers
- Prominent and visible scutes (scales) on the carapace
- Relatively small size compared to other sea turtle species
- Presence of a single pair of prefrontal scales on the head
