- Tailed frog: Long, slender body with a distinct tail, webbed hind feet, and no external eardrums.
- Bell toad: Rounded body shape, warty skin, short hind legs, and a prominent parotoid gland behind the eyes.
- Ribbed toad: Prominent ridges or "ribs" running along the back, warty skin, short hind legs, and a distinct cranial crests.
- Tailed toad: Long, slender body with a distinct tail, warty skin, short hind legs, and a pointed snout.
- Ascaphus trui: Unique reproductive features including internal fertilization and a copulatory organ in males, as well as a lack of a vocal sac in males.
