- Black and gold coloration
- Large size (females can reach up to 1 inch in body length)
- Orb-shaped web with a distinctive zigzag pattern called a stabilimentum
- Long, spiny legs
- Bulbous abdomen
- Presence of a cephalothorax (combined head and thorax)
- Eight eyes arranged in two rows
- Spinnerets at the tip of the abdomen for producing silk
- Presence of a distinctive hourglass-shaped marking on the underside of the abdomen (only in females)
