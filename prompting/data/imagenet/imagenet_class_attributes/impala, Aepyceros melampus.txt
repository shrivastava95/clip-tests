- Medium-sized antelope
- Slender body with long legs
- Short, glossy coat
- Tan to reddish-brown coloration
- White underbelly
- White markings on the face, throat, and buttocks
- Males have long, lyre-shaped horns
- Females do not have horns or have shorter, straighter horns
- Prominent black scent glands on the hind legs
- Distinctive black stripe running along the lower back
- Ears are large and pointed
- Eyes are large and dark
- Muzzle is narrow and pointed
- Tail is short and tufted at the end
