- A flowing and loose fabric
- Draped or wrapped around a person or object
- Typically covers the entire body or a large portion of it
- May have a hood or a collar
- Often made of dark or neutral colors
- May have decorative elements such as embroidery or patterns
- Can have different lengths, from short to floor-length
- May have fastenings such as buttons, clasps, or ties
- Can be made of various materials such as wool, velvet, or silk
- Can be worn for warmth, fashion, or as a costume
