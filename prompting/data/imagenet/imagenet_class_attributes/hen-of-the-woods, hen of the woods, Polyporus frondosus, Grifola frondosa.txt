- Large, fan-shaped fruiting body
- Multiple overlapping layers of fronds
- Fronds are thick, broad, and wavy
- Fronds have a grayish-brown to dark brown color
- Fronds have a velvety or suede-like texture
- Fronds have a white to yellowish-white pore surface
- Pores are small and round
- Pores bruise brown when touched or damaged
- Fruiting body can reach a considerable size, often weighing several pounds
- Typically found growing at the base of trees, particularly oak trees
- Grows in clusters or rosettes
- Fruiting body has a strong, earthy odor
