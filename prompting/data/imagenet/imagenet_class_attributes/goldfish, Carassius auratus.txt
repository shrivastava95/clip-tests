- Distinctive round body shape
- Bright orange or gold coloration
- Fins (dorsal, caudal, pectoral, and anal fins)
- Scales covering the body
- Eyes positioned on the sides of the head
- Small mouth with no teeth
- Gills for respiration
- Two pairs of barbels (whisker-like sensory organs)
- Lateral line running along the sides of the body
- Swims using undulating movements of the body and fins
