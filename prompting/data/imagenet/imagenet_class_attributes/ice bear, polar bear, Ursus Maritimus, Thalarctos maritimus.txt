- Coloration (white fur for polar bear, off-white to yellowish fur for ice bear)
- Size (polar bears are generally larger than ice bears)
- Head shape (polar bears have a more elongated head compared to ice bears)
- Snout length (polar bears have a longer snout compared to ice bears)
- Ear shape (polar bears have smaller, rounder ears compared to ice bears)
- Body shape (polar bears have a more streamlined body compared to ice bears)
- Claw length (polar bears have longer claws compared to ice bears)
- Habitat (polar bears are found in the Arctic, while ice bears are found in the northern parts of Alaska and Canada)
