- Long, thin legs
- Oval-shaped body
- Segmented body structure
- Two distinct body regions (cephalothorax and abdomen)
- Lack of wings
- Lack of antennae
- Lack of venomous fangs
- Lack of silk-producing ability
- Lack of ability to spin webs
- Presence of two eyes
- Presence of pedipalps (short, leg-like appendages near the mouth)
- Presence of chelicerae (pincer-like mouthparts)
- Presence of a pair of long, slender walking legs in front of the body
- Presence of a pair of shorter, stouter walking legs behind the body
- Generally brown or gray coloration
