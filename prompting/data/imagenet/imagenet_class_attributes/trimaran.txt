- Three hulls
- Two outer hulls are typically smaller than the central hull
- Central hull is usually longer and wider than the outer hulls
- Typically has a mainmast and two smaller masts
- Sails attached to the masts
- Typically has a trampoline-like netting between the outer hulls
- Rudder(s) attached to the central hull
- Typically has a cockpit or seating area on the central hull
- Can have outriggers or floats attached to the outer hulls for stability
- Typically has a sleek and streamlined design
