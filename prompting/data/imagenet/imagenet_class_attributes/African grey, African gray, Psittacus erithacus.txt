- Medium to large-sized parrot
- Grey feathers covering most of the body
- White or pale grey face with a black beak
- Red or maroon tail feathers
- Bright red or maroon patch on the upper side of the wings
- White or light grey eye ring
- Intelligent and highly vocal species
- Known for their ability to mimic human speech and sounds
- Strong, hooked beak for cracking nuts and seeds
- Strong, agile feet with sharp claws for climbing and grasping
