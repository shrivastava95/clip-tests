- Medium-sized dog
- Square-shaped body
- Bushy eyebrows and beard
- Pointed ears
- Dark, almond-shaped eyes
- Strong, muscular build
- Docked tail (in some countries)
- Double coat (soft undercoat and wiry topcoat)
- Colors can vary (salt and pepper, black, or black and silver)
- Distinctive "Schnauzer" face with a long muzzle and mustache-like hair on the upper lip
